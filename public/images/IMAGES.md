# Image assets

Place your images in this folder (`public/images/`). Name them exactly as listed below.

## Homepage

These are all optional — the homepage uses live canvas visualizations as the primary visuals.
If you add the headshot, it will appear alongside your name in the hero.

| Filename | What it should be | Dimensions |
|----------|------------------|------------|
| `headshot.jpg` | Portrait photo of Jonathan | Minimum 600×600, square or portrait. JPG or WebP. |

| `robot-sar.jpg` | Photo of the Yahboom RDK X3 SAR rover | 1600×900 (16:9) or wider |

## Tips

- Use `.jpg` for photos, `.png` for anything with transparency, `.svg` for icons/diagrams.
- Keep file sizes under 500 KB — use [Squoosh](https://squoosh.app) to compress.
- WebP is preferred if you have it; the filenames above accept `.jpg` or `.webp`.
